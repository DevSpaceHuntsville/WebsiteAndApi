Donations to DevSpace

There are two options to donate through the buttons on the right. If you use the PayPay Giving Fund button (which is the top one,) you must have a PayPal account to send funds. However, donations received through there have no fees and We get 100% of the money you donate. You will also receive a receipt to make your donation tax-deductible.

If you use the PayPay Direct button (which is the bottom one,) you can make a direct payment without a PayPal account. This method also has the added bonus of releasing the funds to us quicker, but you won't get an instant receipt and we will get charged all the fees. If you include an email address on your donation, we'll reach out to you to produce a receipt. If you do not, then you'll need to email us with the transaction Id.

We're so serious about this, that we have completely revamped the sponsor page for our site. Every person that contributes (that we can manage to track) will get listed in some way on the sponsor page. Once we figure out who you are, we'll get you added to the database.
